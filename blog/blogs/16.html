Kingdom of the Planet of the Apes - 3/5

The film had a slow start but I really liked the whole half an hour or so with the orangutang, I thought that was very interesting how different groups had different interpretations on what Ceasers message was. I don't think I fully understood the ending however, I must watch it again to fully understand it.
