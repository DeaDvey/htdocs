Luxa's grandfather and former king of Regalia, Vikus is a wise and respected figure who serves as a mentor to Gregor. Vikus is calm, rational, and deeply committed to the well-being of his people. He often provides Gregor with important guidance and advice, as well as historical context for the complex political landscape of the Underland.
