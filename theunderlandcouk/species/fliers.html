Fliers (Bats)

The Fliers are close allies of the Humans, and live in caves beside Regalia, they are ruled by Queen Athena (Mother of Princess Nike), Fliers bond to Humans which means they must give their lives to protect one another and usually are bonded due to great friendship. At first sight, Fliers may look like pets of the Humans, but are actually their equals and live side by side with them.
