The Bane


The ultimate antagonist of the series, Gregor refuses to kill him as a pup in The Prophecy of Bane and he later goes on to commit atrocities towards the Nibblers under the pursuasive word of Twirltongue. He is theorised to be inspired by Adolf Hitler, though there are also a lot of differneces
